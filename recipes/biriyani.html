Biriyani Recipe

Home

Description

Biriyani is a popular rice dish that is commonly consumed in India, East African, and other South Asian countries.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Unflavoured yogurt
  • Chicken
  • Spices

Steps to cook

  1. Wash and soak the rice
  2. Marinate the chicken with all the spices and the yogurt
  3. In a separate pan, fry some onions until golden brown and place them in a paper towel to drain excess oils
  4. On the same pan, add the marinated chicken and cook until done
  5. In a large pot, layer the rice and chicken, and add the fried onions on top
  6. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

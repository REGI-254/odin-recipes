Chapati Recipe

Home

Description

Chapati is a type of flatbread that is a staple food in East Africa, India, Pakistan, and other South Asian countries.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Oil or ghee

Steps to cook

  1. Mix the flour with water and knead into a smooth dough
  2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into thin circles
  4. Heat a skillet or tava on high heat
  5. Cook each chapati on both sides until golden brown spots appear
  6. Serve hot with your favorite curry!

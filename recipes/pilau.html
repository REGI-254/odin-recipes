Pilau Recipe

Home

Description

Pilau is a fragrant rice dish that is a staple food in East Africa, India, Pakistan, and other South Asian countries.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat (chicken or mutton)
  • Spices
  • Onions

Steps to cook

  1. Wash and soak the rice
  2. Marinate the meat with all the spices
  3. Fry the onions until golden brown
  4. Cook the marinated meat until done
  5. In a large pot, layer the rice and meat, and add the fried onions on top
  6. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
